## Rhythm
- **Pacing**: fast (16 cuts/min) — beat-driven editing
- **Average cut length**: 3s (±1.5s) — synced to musical phrases
- **Pacing curve**: dynamic — follows the song's energy
- **Silence tolerance**: 0.3s — almost no tolerance, music fills everything

## Transitions
- **Jump cut**: 70% — sharp, energetic transitions
- **Fade**: 5% — only for intro/outro fade from black
- **Dissolve**: 15% — dreamy moments, chorus transitions
- **Wipe**: 10% — creative wipes for visual variety
- **Transition duration**: 0.2s

## Visual
- **Color temperature**: cool — stylized, high-fashion feel
- **Saturation**: 1.3 (punchy, vivid colors)
- **Contrast**: 1.25
- **Brightness**: -0.05 — slightly dark, moody

## Audio
- **BGM**: no — the music IS the content, don't add extra BGM
- **Speech ratio**: 5% — almost entirely music
- **Fade in/out**: 0s

## Subtitles
- **Enabled**: no
- **Position**: bottom
- **Size**: medium

## Patterns
- **Cold open**: no — start with the music from beat one
- **Intro**: none, the song structure IS the structure
- **Segments**: follow verse → chorus → bridge → chorus
- **Outro**: fade to black on final beat

## Rules
- ❌ Never cut in the middle of a musical phrase
- ❌ No subtitles (this is a music video)
- ❌ No additional BGM (the track IS the audio)
- ✅ Cut on the beat whenever possible
- ✅ Use speed ramps for dramatic dance moments
- ✅ High contrast and saturated colors for visual impact
