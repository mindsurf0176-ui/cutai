# Shorts / Reels

> Source: custom — optimized for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels
> Author: CutAI Community
> CutAI EDITSTYLE v1

## Rhythm
- **Pacing**: fast (18 cuts/min) — maximum retention, zero dead time
- **Average cut length**: 2.5s (±1s) — ultra-short attention spans
- **Pacing curve**: fast-slow — hook hard, then vary to keep interest
- **Silence tolerance**: 0.3s — any silence = viewer swipe

## Transitions
- **Jump cut**: 80% — fast, clean energy
- **Fade**: 0%
- **Dissolve**: 5%
- **Wipe**: 15% — creative wipes for visual punch
- **Transition duration**: 0.15s

## Visual
- **Color temperature**: warm — inviting, scroll-stopping
- **Saturation**: 1.25 (pop off the feed)
- **Contrast**: 1.2
- **Brightness**: +0.08

## Audio
- **BGM**: yes, trending / upbeat, 15% volume — always have a backing track
- **Speech ratio**: 70% — fast narration with music underneath
- **Fade in/out**: 0.5s

## Subtitles
- **Enabled**: yes
- **Position**: center — must be visible on mobile
- **Size**: large — readable on small screens
- **Language**: auto

## Patterns
- **Cold open**: yes — THE hook. First 1s determines if they stay
- **Intro**: none — every second counts, skip intros entirely
- **Segments**: one clear topic, no tangents
- **Outro**: CTA in last 2s or loop-friendly ending

## Rules
- ❌ NEVER exceed 60s total duration
- ❌ No scene longer than 5s — keep it moving
- ❌ No slow fades or dissolves — too slow for shorts
- ❌ No intro/brand animation — wastes precious seconds
- ✅ Hook in first 1 second (text overlay or dramatic statement)
- ✅ Subtitles are MANDATORY (80% of shorts are watched muted)
- ✅ End with a loop point or strong CTA
- ✅ Keep aspect ratio 9:16 aware (center-frame important content)
