# Cooking Show

> Source: custom — inspired by Tasty, Maangchi, Baek Jong-won
> Author: CutAI Community
> CutAI EDITSTYLE v1

## Rhythm
- **Pacing**: medium (8 cuts/min)
- **Average cut length**: 7s (±3s) — longer for process shots, shorter for plating
- **Pacing curve**: slow-fast-slow — build up during prep, fast during cooking, slow for reveal
- **Silence tolerance**: 1.5s — cooking sounds are content, not silence

## Transitions
- **Jump cut**: 60% — skip boring parts of prep
- **Dissolve**: 25% — time-lapse feel between cooking stages
- **Fade**: 10% — section breaks (prep → cook → plate)
- **Wipe**: 5%
- **Transition duration**: 0.6s

## Visual
- **Color temperature**: warm — food looks better warm
- **Saturation**: 1.2 (make colors pop, especially greens and reds)
- **Contrast**: 1.1
- **Brightness**: +0.05

## Audio
- **BGM**: yes, acoustic / upbeat, 10% volume — don't overpower sizzling sounds
- **Speech ratio**: 50% — half narration, half cooking ASMR
- **Fade in/out**: 3s

## Subtitles
- **Enabled**: yes
- **Position**: bottom
- **Size**: medium
- **Language**: auto

## Patterns
- **Cold open**: yes — finished dish reveal for 2-3s
- **Intro**: ingredient list overlay (5s)
- **Segments**: prep → cook → plate → taste
- **Outro**: close-up of finished dish, 3s hold

## Rules
- ❌ Don't cut away during the "sizzle moment" — keep cooking action shots
- ❌ No cuts shorter than 2s (cooking needs breathing room)
- ✅ Always show ingredient additions in real-time
- ✅ Keep knife work and plating at normal speed (no speed-up)
- ✅ Include at least one overhead shot if available
