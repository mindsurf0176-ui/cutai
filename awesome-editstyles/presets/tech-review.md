# Tech Review

> Author: CutAI Community

## Rhythm
- **Pacing**: medium (10 cuts/min)
- **Average cut length**: 5s (±2s) — steady, informational pacing
- **Pacing curve**: constant — consistent energy throughout
- **Silence tolerance**: 0.5s — tight editing, no dead air

## Transitions
- **Jump cut**: 90% — clean, professional jump cuts between points
- **Fade**: 5% — only for intro/outro
- **Dissolve**: 3% — b-roll transitions
- **Wipe**: 2%
- **Transition duration**: 0.2s

## Visual
- **Color temperature**: neutral — accurate product colors matter
- **Saturation**: 1.0 (natural, no color enhancement)
- **Contrast**: 1.05
- **Brightness**: +0.02

## Audio
- **BGM**: yes, ambient electronic / minimal, 8% volume — subtle background
- **Speech ratio**: 80% — information-dense narration
- **Fade in/out**: 2s

## Subtitles
- **Enabled**: yes
- **Position**: bottom
- **Size**: medium
- **Language**: auto

## Patterns
- **Cold open**: yes — hero shot of product + one key opinion
- **Intro**: brief (3-5s), product name + category
- **Segments**: design → performance → camera → battery → verdict
- **Outro**: recommendation summary, no lengthy subscribe CTA

## Rules
- ❌ No speed ramps — keep everything at normal speed
- ❌ No heavy color grading — product must look accurate
- ✅ Cut between speaking and b-roll every 8-10s max
- ✅ Keep spec comparisons visible for at least 4s
- ✅ Include close-up product detail shots
